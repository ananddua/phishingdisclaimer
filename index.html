Why This Matters

Phishing remains one of the most common attack vectors used to gain unauthorized access to systems and sensitive data.

Common Signs of Phishing

  • Unexpected or urgent requests
  • Suspicious or mismatched links
  • Generic greetings or unusual formatting
  • Requests for credentials or sensitive information

What You Should Do

  • Always verify the sender before clicking links
  • Avoid entering credentials on untrusted pages
  • Report suspicious emails to IT/Security
  • Stay alert to unusual requests
